Who We Are

What is GDG

Google developers group is a community of enthusiastic Google developers. GDG provides a vast opportunity to those who are interested in Google's developer technology; starting from android, chrome, drive, Google cloud platforms to product API's like the cast API, maps API. The idea of GDG is to bring out talented people with similar interest to the society by engaging them in lot of events.

Google Developers Group - Madurai

Google developers group Madurai supports and welcomes every future developers and aspiring programmers to join hands. GDG Madurai also provides the best platform where one can use his/her interest and talent to learn new things , ideate, innovate, create and bring in the same to the society.

News Feeds

GDG-Madurai encourages and provides opportunity for startup companies in Madurai to use latest Google Technology for their product development. It connects the local entrepreneurs to various startup event like Launchpad, Startup Weekend and Google Entrepreneur program.

GDG-Madurai is organizing the next big developer program on Feb 21 in Madurai. The program focuses on Google Cloud Technolgoy. The program mainly focuses on giving handson for students and profesionals to use the Google App Engine for developing and hosting their web application.

GDG-Madurai is one of the largest developer group in Down South of Tamilnadu. It is started in 31-Jan-2015. Currently we have more than 1000 developers which includes both students and professionals in and around of Madurai. The main objective of the GDG Madurai is to create an eco system for developer in tier 2 and tier 3 cities in Tamilnadu to use Google Technology
