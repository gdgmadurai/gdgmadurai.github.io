Who We Are

What is GDG

Google developers group is a community of enthusiastic Google developers. GDG provides a vast opportunity to those who are interested in Google's developer technology; everything from the android, chrome, drive and Google cloud platforms,to product API's like the cast API, maps API and The idea of GDG is to bring out the people with such a talent to the society by engaging in lot of events.

Google Developers Group - Madurai

Google developers group Madurai supports and welcomes every future developers and aspiring programmer's to join hands. GDG Madurai also provides a best platform where we can use our interest and talent to learn new things everyday, to ideate, to innovate, to create and give it back to the society.

GDG-Madurai is one of the largest developer group in Down South of Tamilnadu. It is started in 31-Jan-2015. Currently we have more than 1000 developer which includes both students and professionals in and around of Madurai. The main objective of the GDG Madurai is to create an eco system for developer in tier 2 and tier 3 cities in Tamil nadu to use Google Technology.

GDG-Madurai encourage and provides opportunity for startup companies in Madurai to use latest Google Technology for their product development. It connect the local entreprenurs to varies startup event like Launchpad, Startup Weekend and Google for Entrepreneur program.
